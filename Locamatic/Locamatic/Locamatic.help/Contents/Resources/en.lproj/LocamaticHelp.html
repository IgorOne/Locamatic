Have you ever wished that your Mac would update its location automatically on the basis of the wireless network that you're using? Perhaps you use your Mac at work, accessing the internet though a proxy server, and at home where you have none. It isn't difficult to select a new location from the Location menu in the Apple menu, but why should you have to? Locamatic will do all this for you!
